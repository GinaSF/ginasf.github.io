About Me

Profile Photo

Gina

Creative Professional

Hi, I’m Gina — a passionate creative with a love for storytelling through design and illustration. I’m currently studying Digital Communication, focusing on graphic design and marketing, where I combine strategic thinking with visual creativity to craft meaningful content and designs.

Before diving into digital communication, I studied illustration and have been drawing since I was 15. What started as a hobby has grown into a lifelong passion and a professional pursuit. Over the years, I’ve developed a unique style that blends artistic expression with a deep understanding of design principles and marketing strategies.

At 27, I’m excited to continue growing as a creative professional, taking on new challenges, and helping brands and individuals tell their stories visually and effectively.

When I’m not working on design projects, you’ll probably find me sketching, exploring new creative trends, or enjoying a good coffee while brainstorming my next idea.

Let’s create something amazing together!
